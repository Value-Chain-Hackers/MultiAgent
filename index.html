Multi-Agent Prompting for In-Game Assistance

In the world of interactive simulations, multi-agent prompting offers a powerful way to enhance in-game learning and decision-making. By using a system of specialized agents, players can receive real-time insights, strategy guidance, and performance feedback tailored to their roles within a game environment. This approach allows for dynamic and personalized support, helping players to understand complex systems, improve their strategy, and make informed decisions on the fly.

Meet "Luca," your dedicated supply chain assistant, powered by multi-agent prompting. Luca provides insights based on contextual data and expert supply chain knowledge, guiding players through complex decisions in simulations like *The Fresh Connection*. Whether you're managing inventory, planning production, or optimizing distribution, Luca is here to assist and enhance your in-game learning experience.
