Teacher A.I. for Inchainge Simulations

1. Project Overview

Objective

To explore the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into Inchainge's simulation games to enhance learning experiences, provide real-time support, automate decision-making processes, and test how well current AI models can navigate and succeed in the games.

Background

Inchainge, a company specializing in supply chain and company simulation games, is keen to leverage recent developments in AI to improve its product offerings. With a strong global presence and a reputation for innovation, Inchainge aims to incorporate AI to offer advanced game-based learning solutions.

Key Questions from The Fresh Connection

  • AI-Powered Game Assistance: Can AI, like ChatGPT, provide real-time support and guidance within the game environment?
  • AI-Based Performance Analysis and Tips: How can AI analyze game outcomes to offer personalized feedback and strategic advice?
  • AI as a Facilitator: What role can AI play in guiding reflection sessions and supporting educators to ensure consistent debriefing?
  • AI for Role Simulation: Can AI simulate management team roles, creating interactive non-player characters (NPCs) to enrich the learning experience?
  • AI to Cheat the Game: Is it possible for AI to independently play the game, make decisions, and effectively "cheat" by optimizing strategies without prior information?

2. Project Scope

AI Integration Goals

  • Real-Time Game Assistance: Develop an AI model capable of providing in-game assistance by responding to player queries, offering hints, and guiding decisions based on game dynamics.
  • Performance Analysis and Feedback: Utilize AI to analyze player performance data and generate customized feedback and tips for improvement.
  • Facilitator Role: Implement AI to act as a facilitator for post-game reflections, aiding teachers and learners in understanding outcomes and enhancing the learning process.
  • Role Simulation: Create AI-driven NPCs that simulate different management roles within the game, enhancing the interactive experience.
  • Automated Gameplay (AI Cheating): Train an AI to autonomously play the game, making strategic decisions to test its ability to outperform human players or provide optimal solutions.

3. Project Phases

Phase 1: Research and Development

  • 1.1 AI Model Selection: Evaluate and select AI models based on their suitability for real-time game assistance and decision-making.
  • 1.2 Game Integration Strategy: Design the architecture for integrating AI with Inchainge's game platforms.

Phase 2: Prototype Development

  • 2.1 AI-Powered Game Assistance Prototype: Develop and test a prototype for real-time AI support.
  • 2.2 AI-Based Performance Analysis Prototype: Create a module that utilizes AI to analyze player performance.
  • 2.3 AI Facilitator Prototype: Implement an AI-driven facilitator for post-game debrief sessions.
  • 2.4 Role Simulation Prototype: Develop AI-driven NPCs to simulate different roles within the game.
  • 2.5 Automated Gameplay (AI Cheating) Prototype: Train an AI model to play the game autonomously.

Phase 3: Pilot Testing and Feedback

  • 3.1 Pilot Testing: Conduct comprehensive pilot testing with diverse user groups.
  • 3.2 Feedback Analysis: Refine AI models based on user feedback and performance data.

Phase 4: Full Implementation

  • 4.1 Final Integration: Fully integrate AI solutions into Inchainge's game platforms.
  • 4.2 Training and Support: Provide training sessions for educators on AI-powered features.

Phase 5: Evaluation and Iteration

  • 5.1 Performance Evaluation: Continuously monitor AI performance and user feedback.
  • 5.2 Iterative Improvements: Improve AI models and integration strategies based on evaluation data.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Project Manager: Oversee the project and manage timelines.
  • AI Development Team: Develop, test, and refine AI models.
  • Game Development Team: Focus on AI integration within the game environment.
  • Educators and Content Specialists: Provide educational input and help design AI interactions.
  • User Experience (UX) Designers: Ensure AI enhances the user experience.
  • Pilot Testing Coordinator: Organize pilot testing and gather feedback.
