About The Fresh Connection

The Fresh Connection is an innovative business simulation game where participants run a virtual juice manufacturing company. Teams of four take on the roles of VP Purchasing, VP Operations, VP Supply Chain, and VP Sales to bring the company back on track. Collaboration and cross-functional decision-making are essential to succeed.

Participants face real-world dilemmas, apply new concepts, and align strategies with operations to achieve the best performance. This game offers a highly engaging and impactful learning experience for professionals and students alike.
